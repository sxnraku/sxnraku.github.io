Football Legacy

A football management game. Start at the bottom of the divisions and build a modest club into a legend — one decision, one season at a time. In Portuguese and English.

Tactics & rotation

Pick the eleven, set the shape, manage fitness and rotate to survive a full campaign.

Balance the books

Every matchday hits the budget. Wages, gates and a board that expects results.

The transfer market

Bids, counter-offers and reputation that opens doors. Land free agents with the Bosman ruling.

Three languages

Play in Português (Portugal), Português (Brasil) or English — switch anytime.
